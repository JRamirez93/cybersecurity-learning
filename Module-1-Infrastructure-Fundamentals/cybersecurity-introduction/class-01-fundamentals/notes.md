# Class 01 – Fundamentals

This file summarizes the key concepts covered in **Class 01 – Fundamentals** within the Cybersecurity Introduction module.

For practical exercises, see the corresponding labs in the `labs` folder.

---

## Topics Covered

1. Cybersecurity vs. Security
2. Areas of Information Security
3. Cybersecurity Status 2024
4. Security Certifications
5. CIA Triad
6. Authentication and Authorization
7. Authentication Concepts
8. Proof of Identity
9. General Password Rules
10. Least Privilege
11. Non-repudiation
12. Implicit Denial
13. Legal and Regulatory Issues
14. Due Care and Due Diligence
15. Information Security Governance

---

## Key Takeaways

- Understanding the fundamentals of cybersecurity and governance.
- Awareness of authentication, authorization, and identity verification.
- Knowledge of key security principles such as CIA triad, least privilege, and non-repudiation.
- Preparedness to apply theory in hands-on labs.
