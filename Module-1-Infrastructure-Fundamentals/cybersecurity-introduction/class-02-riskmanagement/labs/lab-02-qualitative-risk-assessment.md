# Lab 02 – Qualitative Risk Assessment

## Description

This lab focuses on evaluating the security risks of previously identified assets using a qualitative approach.

**Objective:** Perform a qualitative risk assessment of the organization’s assets.

---

## Tasks Performed

1. Identified potential threats for each asset.
2. Evaluated the probability and impact of each threat.
3. Assigned a risk rating to each asset based on probability × impact.

---

## Example Risk Matrix

| Asset           | Threat                 | Probability (1-5) | Impact (1-5) | Risk Level |
|-----------------|----------------------|-----------------|--------------|------------|
| Router 1        | Unauthorized Access   | 3               | 4            | High       |
| PC (Ground floor)| Malware Infection    | 4               | 3            | Medium     |
| Server Web 1    | Data Breach           | 3               | 5            | High       |
| Financial Data  | Accidental Deletion   | 2               | 5            | Medium     |
| Accountant Team | Phishing Attack       | 4               | 4            | High       |
