# Lab 01 – Asset Identification and Classification

## Description

In this lab, I identified and classified the information assets of a company, following a structured approach to assess their importance and confidentiality.

**Objective:** Identify and classify the information assets of an organization.

**Scenario:**  
Tech Account is a modern accounting company that offers automated and digital services to clients from multiple sectors. They use advanced technologies and specialized software to optimize accounting processes while complying with financial and regulatory standards.

---

## Tasks Performed

1. Enumerated all information assets, including hardware, software, data, and personnel.
2. Classified assets into categories: hardware, software, confidential data, public data, human resources, and others.
3. Used an asset classification matrix to assign importance and confidentiality values to each asset.

---

## Example Asset Classification

| Asset           | Category          | Importance | Confidentiality |
|-----------------|-----------------|------------|----------------|
| Router 1        | Hardware         | High       | Medium         |
| PC (Ground floor)| Hardware        | Medium     | High           |
| Server Web 1    | Hardware/Server  | High       | High           |
| Financial Data  | Data Conf.       | High       | High           |
| Accountant Team | Human Resource   | Medium     | High           |

## Deliverables

- List of identified and classified assets
- Completed asset classification matrix
