# Class 02 – Risk Management

This file summarizes the key concepts covered in **Class 02 – Risk Management** within the Cybersecurity Introduction module.

For practical exercises, see the corresponding labs in the `labs` folder.

---

## Topics Covered

1. Assets, Threats, Vulnerabilities, and Risks
2. Risk Management
3. Risk Assessment
4. Risk Response Categories
5. Risk Management Process
6. Exploring Risks and Threats
7. Risk Approaches
8. Attack Surfaces
9. Asset Classification
10. Asset Lifecycle
11. Data Retention

---

## Key Takeaways

- Understanding how to identify and classify organizational assets.
- Knowledge of potential threats and vulnerabilities.
- Awareness of risk evaluation methods and approaches.
- Ability to categorize assets by importance and sensitivity.
- Preparedness to apply theory in hands-on labs.
