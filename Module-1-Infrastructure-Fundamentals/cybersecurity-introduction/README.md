# Cybersecurity Introduction

This section contains my notes, labs, and projects related to the **Cybersecurity Introduction** course.

The goal of this module is to understand the **fundamental concepts of information security**, risk management, security threats, and the role of security operations in modern organizations.

---

# Course Structure

The course is divided into five main classes covering the fundamentals of cybersecurity.

- Proof of Identity

- Non-repudiation

- Due Care and Due Diligence

# Learning Objectives

After completing this module I should be able to:

- Understand the **core principles of cybersecurity**
- Identify **security threats and vulnerabilities**
- Understand **risk management processes**
- Recognize **different access control models**
- Understand the role of **SOC, Red Team, and Blue Team**
